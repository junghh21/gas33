#### 수은주 기압
$$P = \rho gh = 13,595 \times 9.80665 \times 0.76 \approx 101,325 \text{ Pa} (1 \text{ atm})$$
#### 수주
$$\begin{gather}P = \rho \cdot g \cdot h \\
P_{\text{Hg}} = P_{\text{H}_2\text{O}}, \quad \rho_{Hg} \cdot h_{Hg} = \rho_{H_2O} \cdot h_{H_2O}, \quad \\ 13.6 \times 760 \text{ mm} = 1 \times h_{\text{H}_2\text{O}}, \quad h_{\text{H}_2\text{O}} = 10,336 \text{ mm}\end{gather}$$
 $$h = \frac{P}{\rho \cdot g} = \frac{101,325}{1,000 \times 9.80665} \approx 10.332 \text{ m}$$
 $$V = \text{면적}(1\text{ m}^2) \times \text{높이}(10.332\text{ m}) = 10.332\text{ m}^3$$
 $$F = 1,000\text{ kg}_f/\text{m}^3 \times 10.332\text{ m}^3 = 10,332\text{ kg}_f$$
 $$\mathbf{1\text{ atm} = 10,332\text{ kg}_f/\text{m}^2}$$
 
 **1 kgf = 9.80665 N**
